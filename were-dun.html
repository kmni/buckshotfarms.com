Were Dun was selected by Buckshot Farms specifically for producing Half Arabians. His athleticism, gentle spirit, substance, shapely neck, and big eye make him the ideal cross on Arabian mares for today's western show horses.

His offspring have been successful in the western pleasure arena as well as the reining arena, with several offspring succeeding in both disciplines. Their success is attributed to their talent, beauty, and athleticism. You won't find a more versatile, proven sire for Half Arabians.

With less than 30 half arabian foals, the Were Dun presence is well known in the western and reining arena. He is producing a high percentage of winners, and also a high percentage of foals with color.
