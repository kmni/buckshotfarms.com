WELCOME TO BUCKSHOT FARMS

At Buckshot Farms, you will find well bred, talented horses.

We have been breeding and showing for nearly 30 years and have refined our herd to include some of the Arabian industry's finest performers and producers in the performance arena. In recent years, we have started to also breed and show American Quarter Horses, focused on National Reining Horse Association competition.

Our commitment to the highest level of care, upbringing, and training of our horses starts them on their paths of success. We invite you to browse our website, learn more about our farm, horses, staff, and our AQHA stallion, Were Dun, one of the country's leading sires of Half Arabians.
